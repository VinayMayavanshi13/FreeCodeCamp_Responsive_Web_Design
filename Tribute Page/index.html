Elon Musk

The man who is working to make humanity a multiplanet species

Here's a time line of Elon's life:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1983 - At 12 years old, created a game and sold the code for $500.
  • 1995 - Dropped out of his Ph.D. at Stanford after 2 days - launched his first company Zip2.
  • 1999 - Sold Zip2 for $341 Mn - used the money to launch X.com, later merged & became PayPal.
  • 2002 - Self taught rocket scientist, founded SpaceX to make space flights cheaper & colonise Mars.
  • 2006 - Invested $10 Mn in Solar City - today is the largest installer of solar panels in the US.
  • 2007 - Became CEO of Tesla.
  • 2008 - Received $50 Mn in funding,hous before Tesla went bankrupt.
  • 2009 - Debut of Tesla Model S - over 1,000 reservations within a month,became the highest rated car.
  • 2010 - Launched & recovered the first privately owned,reusable spacecraft.
  • 2015 - Launched OpenAI,a non-profit - later partnered with Microsoft for $1 Bn.
  • 2016 - Founded Neuralink & The Boring Company.Plans to send people to Mars by 2029,ticket price $200k.
  • 2021 - SpaceX launches world's first all-civilian space flight. Tesla reaches $1 Tn market cap.
  • 2022 - Net worth: $220 Bn. Richest human. Called off twitter acquisition for $44 Bn.
